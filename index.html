What it does

⚡

Multi-connection downloads

Each file splits into up to 16 parallel segments, with live per-segment progress.

⏸

Pause & resume

Progress is saved to disk — quit the app and pick up right where you left off.

🎬

YouTube & 1800+ sites

Paste a video link and it's routed through yt-dlp automatically, with quality picker, playlists, and subtitles.

🧮

Checksum verification

Optional SHA-256/MD5 check after download, catching corruption that a size check alone would miss.

🌐

Proxy & mirrors

HTTP/SOCKS5 proxy support and multi-source mirrors with automatic failover.

🔄

Auto-updating

Install once — future versions land automatically, no need to redownload.
